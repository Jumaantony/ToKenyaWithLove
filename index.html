WHO WE ARE

To Kenya With Love (TKWL) is a UK Christian charity that works to significantly transform the lives of children in Kisumu, the third largest city in Kenya, where levels of poverty are amongst the highest in the country.


WHAT WE DO


WE OFFER LOVE, SUPPORT AND CARE

Our child sponsorship programme enables us to provide each child with a comprehensive package of care, including quality education, food, clothing and medication. Many of our children have lost both parents and sponsorship helps to keep them with family members, rather than them end up in an orphanage or on the streets.

How Could You Help

Pray Together

Being a Christian Based Organization, prayer is key in changing people's lives. Put us together with our children and the community at large in your prayers.

Give Donation

We call upon well-wishers to and donors to help us meet some of our needs. Donations can be one-time or regular. Donations given help us support needy children as well as paying off staff memebers among other things

Give Hope

Some of our children have gone through difficulties and thus clinging on to hope. You can give them hope by sharing with them some of the life difficulties you've encountered and how you managed to get through them.

Be a Volunteer

In collaboration with our team, you can volunteer to give a hand in reaching out to our needy children, engaging in soci-economic activitism among others.
